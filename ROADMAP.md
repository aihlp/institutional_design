## 🔬 Research Agenda

### Open Questions

#### Theoretical
1. What is the minimal set of primitives needed?
2. How do we formally define institutional boundaries?
3. What mathematical properties characterize stable institutions?
4. How do we measure institutional quality/effectiveness?

#### Empirical
1. Which platforms provide best data for validation?
2. How do we handle cross-platform institutional analysis?
3. What temporal resolution is needed for accurate tracking?
4. How do we validate against ground truth?

#### Methodological
1. Which stationarity tests work best for behavioral data?
2. How do we handle non-stationary environments?
3. What clustering algorithms are most appropriate?
4. How do we quantify uncertainty in forecasts?

#### Ethical
1. How do we prevent misuse for manipulation?
2. What privacy protections are necessary?
3. How do we ensure democratic accountability?
4. What are appropriate use cases vs. red lines?

## ⚠️ Risk Management

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: Methods don't generalize across platforms  
**Mitigation**: Extensive validation studies, diverse datasets  
**Contingency**: Focus on specific domains initially

**Risk**: Computational requirements too high  
**Mitigation**: Optimization, cloud infrastructure, sampling methods  
**Contingency**: Tiered approach with simplified models

### Ethical Risks

**Risk**: Dual-use for manipulation  
**Mitigation**: Ethics review, usage restrictions, transparency  
**Contingency**: Pause deployment, strengthen safeguards

**Risk**: Privacy violations  
**Mitigation**: Aggregation, anonymization, access controls  
**Contingency**: Enhanced protocols, third-party audits

### Sustainability Risks

**Risk**: Funding gaps  
**Mitigation**: Diversified portfolio, lean operations  
**Contingency**: Volunteer model, reduced scope

**Risk**: Key personnel departure  
**Mitigation**: Documentation, cross-training, succession planning  
**Contingency**: Restructure, redistribute responsibilities
